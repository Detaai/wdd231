Discover Kingman

Explore the rich history, vibrant community, and beautiful sites of Kingman, Arizona.

History

Kingman was founded in the late 1800s as a railroad town and has grown into a hub for travelers and businesses in northwest Arizona.

Demographics

  • Population: 32,689
  • Median Age: 44
  • Major Industries: Tourism, Manufacturing, Retail
